Five years up. Never once down. This spring, 53% of Newark Collegiate AP students earned a qualifying score of 3 or higher on at least one Advanced Placement exam. In 2022, that number was 7 percent. Congratulations to the students who showed up on those May mornings, to the teachers who prepared them, and to the families who believed first. We are rooted in excellence, and we are just getting started.
